34. You Like To Stay Organized

Notenik can help you stay organized.

You can use different collections for different kinds of things.

And each collection can be organized in whatever way(s) make the most sense for that kind of data.

You can even have collections of collections. (Grouped into what we like to call a Parent Realm.)
