24. You Want A Proven App

The current version of Notenik, written in Swift, has now been out for about four years.

Notenik has been favorably reviewed in MacLife and MacFormat magazines, receiving four out of five stars (with one star deducted for being Mac-only).

And Notenik currently has a rating of 4.8 out of 5 on the US Mac App Store, based on sixty-plus ratings, with numerous positive reviews.

And even though every submission of a new version allows me, as the developer, to reset the app’s ratings, I have never found any reason to do that.
