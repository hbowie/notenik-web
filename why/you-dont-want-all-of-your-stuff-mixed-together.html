10. You Don't Want All Of Your Stuff Mixed Together

Some apps mix all of your stuff together in a single location.

Notenik supports that.

But it also supports you organizing your stuff into as many different folders (i.e. collections) as you would like.

So you can have different collections for different purposes.

Each with their own kinds of stuff.
