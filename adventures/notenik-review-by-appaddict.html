Notenik Review by AppAddict

Adventure # 35 • Sunday, October 6, 2024

I awoke the other morning to see that Notenik had received a nice review from Lou Plummer on his AppAddict page. I was gratified to read his review, and also happy to make acquaintance with his app reviews more generally. Lou describes himself as a “dedicated Mac user,” and his reviews seem to mostly focus on macOS apps, which is in alignment with my own interests. I look forward to reading more of his pieces, and perhaps discovering some new Mac apps to add to my own toolbox.
