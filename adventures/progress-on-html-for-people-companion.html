Adventures with Notenik

Progress on HTML for People Companion

Adventure # 39 • Wednesday, December 11, 2024

Notenik version 15.9.0 is now available from the Mac App Store, and is chock full of improvements (including a few fixes).

As with our prior adventure, many of the enhancements here were inspired by working my way further through Blake Watson’s excellent web book HTML for People.

Watson’s book is for beginners, but I keep learning new things, and seeing new ways to make Notenik easier to use, and this release includes more of these sorts of refinements.

And while my Notenik companion to HTML for People is not yet complete, I think it is now far enough along to make it available for any interested reviewers.

I’ve decided to publish it at notenik.app/web-intro.

If you’re at all interested, I’d love to hear any feedback you might have.

And, as always, bug reports can be filed and enhancement requests can be submitted at discourse.notenik.app.
