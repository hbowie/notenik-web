1.2 A Sample Collection to Work With

As you work your way through this Master Class, you may find it useful to have a sample Collection you can inspect and experiment with as you learn to apply new concepts and features. A good starting point can be generated by creating a New Collection, and then selecting 12 - Website as your starting Collection type. You will then end up with a folder containing examples of much of what will be discussed in this class. See the README.md file, or the relevant Knowledge Base page for more info about this helpful starter pack.
