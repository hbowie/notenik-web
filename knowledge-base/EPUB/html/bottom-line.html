4. Is Notenik Right for You? ↑

4.26 Bottom Line

Notenik is admittedly not for everyone, and it is not intended to be.

There are lots of good reasons to choose one of the other apps that share this note-taking space.

On the other hand, I’ve received lots of positive feedback about Notenik, including a recent four-star review in the MacLife/MacFormat magazines, and so I think others might wish to give it fair consideration before choosing an app for their own use.
