4. Is Notenik Right for You? ↑

4.1 Supported Platforms

Notenik is, rather unapologetically, a Mac App. It is available only on macOS, from the Mac App Store. So if your primary platform for working with your Notes is something other than a Mac, then Notenik will not be for you.

That being said, Notenik is as open and non-proprietary as I can make it, and it integrates with other apps, such as Drafts, so it’s entirely possible to capture your notes on other platforms, and then send them over to Notenik once you’re back on your Mac.

I’ll also point out that the Mac has been undergoing quite a resurgence lately, so if you were ever thinking about switching, most observers agree that now might be a good time.
