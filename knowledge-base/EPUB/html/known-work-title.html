14.2.2.4 Known Work Title

When a Work Title field is available for a Collection, then then a special variable name of known-work-title can be used in a Merge Template. This derived variable will simply be equal to the Work Title, unless the Work Title is ‘unkown’, in which case the Known Work Title will be blank.
