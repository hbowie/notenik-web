7.5. Education

This will be our fourth and final note for this collection.

Give it a Title of Education and a Seq of 4.

Then add your significant educational accomplishments in the Body.

Here’s a sample entry for me. 

**B.A. in English Literature**, 1973 \
*Ann Arbor, Michigan*
