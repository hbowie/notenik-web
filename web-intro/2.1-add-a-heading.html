2. Add content to your website ↑

2.1. Add a heading

Go back to your Index note within your pages collection, and click on the Edit tab again.

Make sure the Header field of your first note contains the possessive form of your name, followed by “Homepage.”

For example, mine would read Herb's Homepage.

Now save your edits by clicking on the Display tab.

Now you’ll see your page heading entered into your Header field.
