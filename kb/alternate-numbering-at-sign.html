9.3.8 Alternate Numbering - At Sign

The @ at sign can be used to display an integer in an alternate numbering format, with the format depending on the following letter:

Letter Numbering Format
A uppercase letters
a lowercase letters
I uppercase roman numerals
i lowercase roman numerals
