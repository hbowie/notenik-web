9.11.4 Minutes to Read

The phrase Minutes to Read can be used as both a label and a type.

A Collection should only have one field of type minutes-to-read.

The value for this field will be calculated automatically by Notenik, based on the number of words found in the Body field, figuring 225 words per minute, and rounding to the nearest whole number.
