12.3 Variable Modifiers ↑

12.3.18 Initial Case - I

When the letter ‘I’ immediately follows the Lowercase Modifier - L or the Uppercase Modifier - U, then the case transformation (lower or upper) will only be applied to the initial letter of the variable.
