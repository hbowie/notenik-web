5.4 Built-In Starter Packs

Once you reach the Fields tab in the Create a New Collection window, you will see the question “What sort of Collection would you like?”, followed by a drop-down menu listing your options.

The built-in Starter Packs will appear at the top of this list, before any custom packs that have been added to the iCloud Drive > Notenik > Starter Packs folder.

These built-in Starter Packs are described below.
