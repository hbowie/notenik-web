9.2.3.9 Work Rights Slug

When the Work RIghts and Work Rights Holder fields are specified for a Collection, then a special variable name of work-rights-slug can be used in a Merge Template in order to obtain a nicely formatted rights line – typically, a copyright notice.

In such a case, an accompanying Date field will be used as the initial publication date for the referenced work.
