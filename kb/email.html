9.10 Fields for Contacts ↑

9.10.1 Email

The word Email can be used as both a label and a type.

An Email field is meant to contain a single email address.

An Email field will be treated much like a Link field; the mailto: URI scheme will be placed before the email address itself in order to form the full link.
