12.5 Support

If you have a question or problem that cannot be resolved through reference to the Notenik Knowledge Base, then please feel free to drop me a note at support @ notenik.app, or to make use of the Notenik Discourse Forum, and I’ll try to respond to your inquiry as quickly as possible.
