11 Common Use Cases

This section provides focused examples of how to put together Notenik’s various capabilities in pursuit of specific goals.

These articles generally assume that you have already downloaded and installed Notenik from the Mac App Store.

They also assume that you have read and understood the basics of creating and tailoring a Collection of Notes, as well as how to perform some common tasks.

And, finally, they generally assume that you’re using the Notenik Markdown parser.
