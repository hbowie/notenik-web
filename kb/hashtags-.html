9.3.9 Hashtags - #

This modifer is meant to be used specifically with the Tags field.

It will convert a note’s tags into hashtags and place them one after another on a single line, separated by spaces. Tags will be formatted into CamelCase, following this guidance.

Additionally, one may suppress certain tags from inclusion by placing them after a minus sign (‘-’). Multiple tags to be excluded may be separated by commas or semicolons.

One may also add certain fixed tags by placing them after a plus sign (‘+’). No case conversion will be done on tags to be added. The tags to be added will precede the specific tags for a note.
