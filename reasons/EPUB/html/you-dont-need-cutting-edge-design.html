22. You Don't Need Cutting Edge Design

Notenik will probably never receive an Apple Design Award.

But we’re not losing any sleep over that.

Because Notenik is primarily a tool for storing, organizing and publishing chunks of text and – frankly – you probably don’t want to see a bunch of whizzing graphics and fancy effects when you are trying to work with down-to-earth text.

And so Notenik will never be a showcase for the latest Apple design techniques.

If you’re ok with that, then so are we.
