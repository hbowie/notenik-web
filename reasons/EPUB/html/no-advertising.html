Reasons to Use Notenik ↑

4. No Advertising

When we say Notenik is free, we really mean it.

So no advertising either, in the app or on the website.

And no popups asking you to subscribe to anything.

We don’t want anything like that to ever get in the way of the use and enjoyment of Notenik, for any user.
