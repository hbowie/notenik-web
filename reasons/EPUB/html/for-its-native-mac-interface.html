20. For Its Native Mac Interface

Many apps these days are written to look pretty much the same whether you are using them in a web browser, on a Mac, on a PC, or on an Android phone. And we understand the economic factors that are driving companies to make these sorts of compromises.

But Notenik uses a native Mac user interface. And so, if you’ve been using a Mac for any length of time, you’ll feel right at home when you fire up Notenik.
