19. You Want Other Metadata Too

Tags are fine, but what about other sorts of fields you might want to use to categorize, filter, sort and annotate your Notes?

Notenik let you add any number of additional fields to each Note in a Collection.

These additional fields are often referred to as metadata.

And in order to store metadata in your text files, you can choose between the MultiMarkdown metadata format, the YAML frontmatter format, and Notenik’s own easy-to-read, easy-to-write format.

So Notenik goes above and beyond to make your notes interoperable with other note-processing systems.
