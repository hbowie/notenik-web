5. It's Open-Source

You may be a fervent supporter of open-source software.

Or you may have never looked at a line of computer source code in your life, and never used a piece of software you didn’t pay for.

We’re good with both approaches.

But just so you know, every line of application code that goes into Notenik is stored on GitHub, which means anyone can view it, or even make a copy and modify it.

Which means fewer worries about the app suddenly disappearing without a trace, never to be updated again.
